Hero Trails

A small and simple Visual Card driven TTRPG for small ages all the way down to 6 years old. This game system is being designed because i have small children myself and want to introduce them to TTRPG faster than normal. So why not use Cards with a visual style to help drive home a story and have simple rules to control event and encounters.
