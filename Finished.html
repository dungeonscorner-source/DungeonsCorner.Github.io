Escape the Nursing Home

Escape the nursing home is my first small passion project. I have always used this system as a one-shot to introduce people to TTRPG or just to shake a group together and break the cycle a little. It is a simple rules lite system with a lot of improvisation and tons of good laughter, chaos and dark humor.

You are 90+ years old. You don’t remember how you got here. You are not allowed to leave. The staff are polite. The guards are strong and maybe armed. The doors are locked “for your own safety”. Somewhere between routine checkups, medication schedules, and friendly smiles, you see an opportunity to make a break for it.

You remember one thing: Get out.
